Calculator Functionality

Our Persona 3 FES Triangle Fusion Calculator simplifies persona fusion. Here's how it works:

  • 1. Select Persona Names: Choose the names of three personas from the dropdown menus.
  • 2. Choose Levels: Select the levels for each persona from the available options.
  • 3. Automatic Arcana: The Arcana is automatically displayed based on your selections.
  • 4. Initiate Fusion: Click the "Fuse!" button to start the fusion process.

How to Use the Calculator

Here's a simple guide to utilizing the Persona 3 FES Triangle Fusion Calculator:

  • 1. Select Persona Names: Pick the names of the three personas you want to fuse.
  • 2. Choose Levels: For each persona, select the appropriate level from the dropdown menu.
  • 3. Automatic Arcana: The calculator will display the Arcana for each persona automatically.
  • 4. Initiate Fusion: Click the "Fuse!" button to perform the fusion.
