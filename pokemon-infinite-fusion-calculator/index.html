How To use

Have you ever wondered what it would be like to fuse two Pokémon together? Well, now you can find out with the Pokémon Infinite Fusion Calculator! This nifty tool allows you to fuse any two Pokémon together to create a completely new creature.

To get started, simply choose two Pokémon that you want to fuse together. Once you have chosen your Pokémon, click the "Fuse" button to generate your new fused Pokémon.

Once the fusion process is complete, you will be able to see a picture of your new Pokémon and learn more about its attributes. This includes its type, stats, and abilities.

You can also use the calculator to generate random Pokémon. This is a great way to find new and interesting Pokémon that you might not have thought of before.

So what are you waiting for? Start fusing Pokémon today and see what you can create!
